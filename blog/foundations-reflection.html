My participation in group discussions with video 'ON' is improving. I'm only a little weird now. 😂

I am allowing stranger's inside my circle. My circle is getting bigger.

I still have a long way to go to improve personally. But hey...it won't happen overnight but it will happen.

The values you have instilled within you are how you approach each learning process whilst programming.

The empathy you show to yourself and others have an impact on you personally.

Knowing your limits and the ability to push through them has an amazing feeling.

Or maybe to make us employable in any industry not just in a coding industry but anywhere.

Core learning is definately not a waste of time. Actually helps to give me a break from programming brain-mush.
